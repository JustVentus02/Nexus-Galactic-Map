Udvaris System

Capital of the Artemis Coalition

For over two centuries, the Udvaris system has stood as a seat of galactic power. Originally colonized during the Aegis Empire's (AGI) exodus from Sol in 3023.

After the Aegis Empire fell during the Aegis Schism (3223-3243) the newly formed Artemis Coalition (ARC) reclaimed Udvaris as its capital due to its established infrastructure, strategic location, and symbolic importance.
